The GitHub repository vivizzz007/vivi-music hosts Vivi Music, an open-source Android music streaming app. It enables users to play YouTube/YouTube Music content without ads, supports background playback, downloads for offline use, synchronized lyrics, audio normalization, silence skipping, and more. It also features a dynamic Material 3 theme and localization.
